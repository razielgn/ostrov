# Numbers

## Implemented

* Signed (64 bits) integers.

## Missing

* Unlimited sized signed integers.
* Limited sized rationals.
* Unlimited sized rationals.
* IEEE-754 floating point numbers.
* Complex numbers.
* Exactness.

# Parser

## Implemented

* Identifiers (atoms).
* Signed integers.
* Lists.

## Missing

* Booleans.
* Characters.
* Strings.
* Dotted-lists.
* Lists with `[]`.
* Vectors.
* Bytevectors.
* Quoting/unquoting.
